Chitauri spaceship

Chitauri spaceship is the main mode of transportation used by the alien race Chitauri They are commanded by the Chitauri Command Ship, a massively large ship. The hulls are laced with vibranium and are virtually indestructible. They can hide from normal radar and require special technology to track.

Qship

Q-Ships are massive spaceships used by Thanos and his Army. These ring-shaped vessels were stored inside the even larger Flagship, and Thanos deployed them during his invasions.
